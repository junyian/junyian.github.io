My first post!
Nothing much to say. I started this blog because recently [Fliermate][fliermate-gh]
introduced [Flare-on][flareon] reverse engineering challenges to me. And I have 
not done any reverse engineering for more than a decade, so I thought I'd try 
the previous challenges, take this year's challenge, and just see how far I
could go.

Happy to say that I managed to complete the challenge, with some help from a
good [friend][klks84-tw] whom I've didn't keep in touch for many years till 
the Flare-on 2021 challenge happened.

My next few posts will probably revolve around the Flare-on challenges, with
maybe some unrelated stuff in between. I'm still playing around with Github
pages and Jekyll in the meantime.
